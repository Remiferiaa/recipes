Pancakes

A simple yet well known dish

Dish that is perfect for the morning

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Mix flour,sugar,baking powder and salt
  2. Make a well in the centre, pour in milk egg and oil. Mix until smooth
  3. Heat pan to middle heat
  4. Pour 1/4th cup of batter per pancake into the pan .Brown on both sides and serve hot.
