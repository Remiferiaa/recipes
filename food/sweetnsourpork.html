Sweet and sour pork

A delicious plate of food that goes well with rice

Classical chinese food that combines crispy fried pork pieces with a rich sweet and sour sauce

Ingredients

Main ingredients

  • 10 oz of pork shoulder butt
  • 4 oz of pineapple
  • 1 oz of red bell pepper
  • 1 oz of yellow bell pepper
  • 1 oz of green bell pepper
  • 1 oz of red onion
  • 1 oz of yellow onion
  • oil for frying

Pork Marinade

  • 1 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1/2 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce
  • 1 oz of yellow bell pepper
  • 1 oz of green bell pepper

Sauce

  • 4 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 4 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoon of vinger
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoon of water

Instructions

  1. Cut and marinate the pork
  2. Cut the vegetables and pineapple
  3. Create the sauce
  4. Coat the cut pork in flour
  5. Fry the pork
  6. Cook the other ingredients, add in the sauce and mix it
